About

WinnerOdds is an Artificial Intelligence system developed in 2014 by Spanish engineer, professor, and bettor Miguel Figueres. After discovering that, with strong mathematical foundations, it was possible to beat the bookmakers over the long run—without human intervention in bet selection—Miguel built WinnerOdds for himself and a close group of friends.

The key to WinnerOdds lies in identifying a high volume of value bets—on average, 10 per day—that allow users to rotate their entire bankroll up to 4 times per month.

Civil Engineer and Associate Professor at the UPV, specializing in Neural Networks and Artificial Intelligence.
